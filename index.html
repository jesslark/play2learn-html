Awesome Anagram Hunt

An anagram is a word made by rearranging the letters of another word. The object of the Anagram Hunt game is to find all the anagrams for a given word. It's a super awesome way to play with words!

Megacool Math Facts Practice

This is a simple game for practicing basic math. Problems include addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. The division facts are all without remainders. Math is megacool!
